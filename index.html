About Us

Welcome to National Library, the premier digital study space and reading environment in Maharajganj, Azamgarh.

"The very first premium library in the entire Maharajganj region."

Strategically located near the Post Office, we are dedicated to providing aspirants and students with a distraction-free, fully air-conditioned environment. We understand the value of deep focus, which is why our library features ergonomic seating, high-speed 5G Wi-Fi, pin-drop silence, RO water, daily newspapers, and 24/7 CCTV surveillance to ensure utmost security and comfort.
